Technical Capabilities

Engineered for Performance

Every capability is designed for scale, reliability, and seamless integration with existing enterprise infrastructure.

Sub-10ms Policy Evaluation

OPA/Rego rules compiled to WebAssembly and evaluated at the edge. No round-trip latency for critical security decisions. Policies update in real-time without service restart.

WASM compilation • Zero cold starts

Multi-Region Redundancy

Active-active deployment across regions with automatic failover. Data is replicated synchronously within regions and asynchronously across regions for disaster recovery.

99.99% SLA • RPO < 5s
